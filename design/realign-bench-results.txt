## Interpretation (2026-07-13)

Setup: 200 seeds, budget 4000, three shrink-replay realignment
policies. "minimal" = reached the exact stated ideal (the canonical
shortlex-smallest example). "avg-size" = mean failing magnitude
(a+b, or c). replays/tests = mean generation runs and test executions
per found case (the true cost). misaligned = total proposals whose
replay hit a kind mismatch.

Findings:

1. FREE WHEN ALIGNED. Baseline never misaligns; `both costs exactly
   the same as consume/freeze (41.8 replays, 39.0 tests, identical to
   the digit). The misalignment gate means aligned shrinking pays
   nothing.

2. NEVER WORSE, SOMETIMES BETTER. `both matches the better fixed
   policy on every generator. On freeze-favouring it reached the
   canonical shortlex-minimal example 136/200 vs consume's 92/200
   (+48%): a real quality win, because that ideal lives in the
   shape whose tag choice is smaller, and consume loses shrink
   progress crossing to it while freeze/both carry it across.

3. TINY COST. Extra replays only on the misaligned minority: +0% on
   baseline, +0.6 replays (+3%) on freeze-favouring, ~noise on
   consume-favouring. Tests essentially unchanged.

4. THE PASS SUITE REPAIRS MAGNITUDE. All policies reach avg-size 100
   everywhere, and the consume-favouring generator ties at 200/200:
   redistribute + per-choice minimization drive the values to the
   boundary regardless of what a bad realignment scrambled, given a
   generous budget. So `both's win is specifically about reaching the
   canonical minimal FORM more often on shape-changing generators, NOT
   about failing magnitude (which the other passes already handle).

Bottom line: try-both is sound, free when aligned, cheap (~0-3%) when
not, and never underperforms either fixed policy. Its concrete payoff
is more canonical-form minimal examples on generators that change
shape under shrinking (bind/union/tag), which are exactly the hard
cases the tape engine exists for. It is a low-cost, low-risk,
occasionally-meaningful addition rather than a dramatic win.
